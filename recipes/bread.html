Homemade Bread

Description

Homemade BreadThis bread machine recipe is easy and foolproof. It makes a very soft and tasty loaf of bread with a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Bread Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast in the pan of the bread machine.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients
  3. Select the Basic or White Bread setting, then press Start
  4. Cool in the pan for five minutes after the cycle is done, then cool on a rack.
